Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about using the QPo rider app.

How do I sign up for the rider app?

You can sign up by entering your phone number and verifying it with an OTP sent to your mobile.

How do I find nearby share autos?

The app uses GPS technology to display all nearby share autos on a map, allowing you to locate one easily.

What is a hotspot?

Hotspots are the pick-up or drop points defined by QPo. They are usually prominent locations like bus stops, restaurants, schools, colleges, and office parks.

How do I find the nearest hotspot?

The nearest hotspots from all routes will always be shown distinctly.

What should I do if I encounter any issues with the app?

You can email our customer support through the app for any assistance or to report issues.

Does the app work in areas with poor network coverage?

The app requires an internet connection to provide real-time updates. In areas with poor network coverage, some features may be limited.

How can I provide feedback about my usage experience?

The support team of QPo will reach out to you on a timely basis to collect feedback.

Is the rider app free to use?

Yes, the app is free to download and use.

How do I pay for the ride?

All payments has to be done directly to the driver and QPo does not have any say in the fare pricing as per this version.
