About Us

QPo is all about hassle-free daily commutes

Launched in early 2024 by Unovay Technologies Private Limited, QPo is designed to enhance the daily commuting experience by making it more comfortable and efficient. Recognizing the challenges faced by daily commuters who rely on overcrowded and often unreliable public transportation options, QPo aims to provide a better alternative.

By focusing on ride-sharing, QPo has introduced services using share autos in select areas, offering an organized, affordable, and more dependable travel solution.

The platform addresses the common issues of long waits and overcrowding, providing a convenient option for those seeking a smoother daily commute. With its emphasis on quality, predictability and efficient, QPo is set to revolutionize the way people navigate their daily journeys, without the stress and inconvenience typically associated with public transport.
